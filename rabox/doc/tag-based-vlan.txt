Why we need tag-based vlan
--------------------------

The major difference is that tag-based VLAN can not only effect on
single, but also when more than one Switch is connected together. Since
port-based VLAN function on a Switch can identify each VLAN by the port
mapping table, it does not attach any VLAN information on that packet. Once 
packets go through another Switch, the other one will not know which VLAN they
belong to.
This is the reason why 802.1q tag-based VLAN is used.
