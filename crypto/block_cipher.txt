MODES OF OPERATTION
===================

ECB: Electronic CodeBook, simplest mode and not require IV.
CBC: Cipher-Block Chaining, encryption is sequential.
PCBC: Propagating/Plaintext CBC.
OFB: Output FeedBack
CFB: Cipher FeedBack
IAPM
CCM
EAX
GCM
OCB
CTR/CM/SIC/ICM: CounTeR
